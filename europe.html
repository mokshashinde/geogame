    EUROPE

Europe is a continent located entirely in the Northern Hemisphere and mostly
in the Eastern Hemisphere. It comprises the westernmost peninsulas of the
continental landmass of Eurasia, it shares the continental landmass of
Afro-Eurasia with both Asia and Africa and is bordered by the Arctic Ocean
to the north, the Atlantic Ocean to the west, the Mediterranean Sea to the south,
and Asia to the east.
