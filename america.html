    AMERICA

The Americas, which are also collectively called America,are a landmass
comprising the totality of North and South America. The Americas make
up most of the land in Earth's Western Hemisphere and comprise the New World.
Along with their associated islands, the Americas cover 8% of Earth's total surface
area and 28.4% of its land area.
